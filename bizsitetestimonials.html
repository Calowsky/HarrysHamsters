Harry's Hamsters: Testimonials


Boris Bergeron, owner of Boris's Buffet of Brighton says that one of the reasons people always come back to his restuarant is because of the classic hamster taste in his soups that everyone is always ranting and raving about. According to Boris, people have come from as far as Newton Massachusetts to try his famous hamster soup. Boris says that he uses Hamster for soups because boiling them in water is the only way to unlock that famous hamster flavor.

"Harry has by far the best hamsters I've worked with!"" -Boris Bergeron



Sally Serpentine, owner and founder of Sally's Sausages, credits Harry's Hamsters as the ingredient that saved her company. Sally's Sausages, located in Somerville, was once one of the most popular restuarants in town. However, in the mid 2000's the keto diet started getting more and more popular, and no one wanted to get a sausage without a bun. Her business started drying up, and she was months behind on rent. However, when she put hamster-werst on her menu the entire sitation turned around over night. Just two weeks after putting hamster in her sausages, Sally had lines down the block of hungry customers, eagerly awaiting her now-famous snack.

"I can't believe I ever made sausages without Harry's help! -Sally Serpentine
